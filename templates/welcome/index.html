WHAT'S THIS WEBSITE ABOUT?

S.I.P 's primary goal is to help make learning in the university easier by bringing every materials and tools you ever need to pass any course right in your phone. We have all been in a situation where we needed to access past questions  or get handouts and we go to where its been sold and we get that heartbreaking news that the material we are looking for is out of stock. SIP helps solves all of that for you it will help do all the dirty work and save you the stress of roaming up and down around school looking for handouts or past questions. The website is also there to help you with other reading materials like textbooks in   PDF  format, intensive video tutorials and many more cool stuffs that will help you towards your journey as a college student........
